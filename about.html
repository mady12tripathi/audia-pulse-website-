About Audia+

Audia+ is redefining the wireless audio experience with AI-powered personalization. Our mission is to build sound that adapts to *you*. This brand is built by creators, for creators — audiophiles, athletes, and tech lovers alike.

We believe no two ears are alike. That’s why we created OmniWave™ AdaptIQ – a smart sound system that maps your hearing and environment for ultra-personal audio.
